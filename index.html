Meet The Puppet Players

The Puppet Players began back in 1986 with the idea that puppet shows should be fun, creative, and accessible to all ages. We bring professional shows to elementary schools, libraries, daycares, preschools, birthday parties, churches, assisted living communities, and more - from Ogden to Payson. The colorful characters are hand puppets, and the stories are full of imagination, music, and meaningful lessons for every audience. Every show includes a hands-on portion where members of the audience get to participate and be part of the puppet fun.

  • One puppet show is $110 - regardless of group size.

  • Prices are slightly increased for weekends or evenings.
