Meet The Puppet Players

The Puppet Players began back in 1986 with the idea that puppet shows should be fun, creative, and accessible to all ages. We bring professional shows to elementary schools, libraries, day cares, preschools, birthdays, churches, residential care and more from Ogden to Provo. The colorful characters are hand puppets, and the stories are full of imagination, music and kid-friendly lessons. The programs also contain a "hands-on" portion, where members of the audience get to participate and be part of the puppet fun.

Frequently Asked Questions

Depending on availability, at least 24hrs are needed to book a show or workshop, though we encourage booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for our summer and winter holiday months to ensure your preferred date and time are available.
One puppet show is $110- regardless of group size.
A one hour workshop is $200 for up to 20 attendees. Each additional attendee is $5.
Prices are slightly increased for weekends or evenings
Discounts are available for multiple bookings, payment in advance, or combinations with workshops!
Please reach out with any questions, concerns or ideas. We are very happy to work with you.
Each show has an average run time of 30-45 minutes, which includes a "hands-on" portion, where members from the audience get to participate and be part of the puppet fun.
Let us know of any specific time requirements. We can adjust the show to best fit your centers needs.
We travel to all sorts of events and centers to put on puppet shows. These include, but are not limited to; Daycares, Preschools, Elementary schools, Afterschool Programs, Church Activities, Summer Camps, Care Centers, Rec Centers, Libraries, Medical Centers, Birthday Parties, Arts in the Parks events, Farmers markets, Resorts, Goblin caves, Underwater Scuba Concerts… Anywhere our little stage can fit, we’ll be there.
We can accept cash, check, card, Venmo and PayPal. Payment is not due until the scheduled show date or within the two following weeks.
We know things happen! We request a minimum of 24 hours before your show time to cancel or reschedule your puppet show. There is no cancellation fee, however, if a puppeteer shows up at a scheduled show without prior warning of cancellation from the client, the client will be charged 20% of the show price.
